Melissa Ramos

Psychology

Melissa is a fourth-year Psychology major at the University of California, Riverside. She is a very actively involved and determined student that is an aspiring veterinarian. Through this experience she has developed great leadership skills through her roles as an officer in school clubs, research labs, and her role as a veterinarian assistant. Melissa's passion for volunteering has helped her develop social and communication skills that will be a great asset for her future career. She is seeking a position that will further enhance and develop a new set of skills.

Melissa previously interned and worked at Melrose La Brea Animal Hospital in Los Angeles. She hopes to apply everything she learned towards exotic and domestic animals. Melissa studied abroad in Ireland for a semester and now strives to return to Europe to further her education. Her hobbies include hiking, drawing, and exploring! Melissa is extremely passionate about helping animals and people.

Melissa Ramos is a first generation college student, meaning she is the first to attend a university and soon will be the first to achieve a Bachelors Degree. With this degree she hopes to obtain a Masters in Psychology, specifically in Behavior. She will use this to become a certified ABA therapist, meanwhile she works to strengthen her resume and further her education abroad.
